Privacy Policy — KoreanCards

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. General

KoreanCards ("App") is developed by Gulchokhra Medzhidova. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

The App may collect: email address and name upon registration, learning progress (studied words, statistics, streak), voice recordings for pronunciation practice (stored locally on your device only), notification reminder time (stored locally).

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to provide app functionality and personalise your learning experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted.

5. Permissions

The App requests microphone access to record your pronunciation and notification permission for daily reminders. Both permissions are optional.

6. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: gulchokhramedzhidova@gmail.com
